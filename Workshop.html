    Homefitness Equipment Service and Maintenance

    Our technicians are knowledgeable and trained to work with all types of at-home exercise equipment and can assist with various services, including repair, preventive maintenance, assembly, and disassembly.

    In Home Gym Repair

    With regular use, your home treadmill, elliptical or exercise bike is bound to need minor—or not so minor—repairs at some point. After years of wear and tear, parts break down, electrical problems surface, and sometimes major components need servicing or replacing.

    Our techs routinely fix many common repair requests for our residential customers, including but not limited to:

    • Control/circuit board and motor repairs
    • Parts replacements for belts, pedals, seats, benches, etc.
    • Diagnostics for mechanical and/or electrical issues

    In Home Fitness Equipment Assembly & Disassembly

    Proper assembly is key to making sure your exercise equipment is safe to use and won't break down once you start using it. If you recently bought a new piece of equipment and need help getting it set up, get in touch with our assembly experts. Our techs can assemble a variety of equipment—from standard machines like treadmills and ellipticals, to larger and more complex setups like multi-station weight machines.

    In Home Gym Maintenance

    Preventive maintenance can help keep your fitness equipment performing in top shape for many years to come. Just like your car, exercise machines benefit from regular maintenance checks and tune ups to make sure everything is running as it should. They can also help spot small, easy-to-fix problems before they become bigger, more costly problems in the future.Our techs provide one-time maintenance visits and ongoing maintenance contracts for both residential and commercial customers.
